This Tic-Tac-Toe game is my final project. They're are many options to choose from on the opening scene,
where you can choose 1 or 2 players, light or dark mode, and choose X or O. You can also reset the score
pressing the score button on the opening scene. 2 player mode allows you to play against another user on the
same device. 1 player mode can be played on easy or hard mode, with easy mode being random cpu moves, and hard
mode having the cpu prevent you from winning. Enjoy playing Tic-Tac-Toe!
